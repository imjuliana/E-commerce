About Julia's Craft

Welcome to Julia’s Craft a simple hobby that began in September 2025 when I started making satin flowers as a gift for someone special.

People appreciated my work and encouraged me to turn it into a small business, and from there, it slowly grew.

Today, every handmade satin flower is crafted with the same passion and intention to bring joy, beauty, and a personal touch to any moment.
